Features

Everything you need to
think more clearly.

4 Node Types

Concept, Question, Note, and Snapshot nodes. Each with a distinct color, icon, and purpose in your thinking map.

Visual Connections

Drag connector dots to link nodes with smooth bezier curves. Single-click to select, double-click to delete, or use the unlink button.

YouTube Timestamps

Capture any moment from a YouTube video as a node. Click the timestamp to jump back to that exact point in the video instantly.

Rich Study Notes

Built-in notepad with bold, italic, lists and headings. Auto-saves every keystroke. Your sidebar for structured text notes.

Undo / Redo

Full undo/redo history with up to 50 steps. Ctrl+Z and Ctrl+Y work as expected. Never lose your work accidentally.

Zoom & Minimap

Zoom in/out with scroll wheel or buttons. Space+drag to pan. Always-visible minimap shows your position on the infinite canvas.

6 Color Themes Per Node

Assign any of 6 accent colors to each node. Color-code by topic, priority, or category to make your canvas instantly scannable.

Image Upload

Upload images directly as nodes. Drag, resize, and connect them just like any other node to add visual context to your canvas.

Export Canvas

Export your entire canvas as a high-quality PNG image. Share your thinking maps or archive them for later reference.
